What is casual style of clothes?

Casual style is what you might wear on the weekend.

Think jeans, comfortable T-shirts, and sneakers or flat boots. Both streetwear and sporty style might be considered types of casual styles, but casual style can also lean preppy or hip.

History

In the 50s of the last century, a new Teddy-boys youth movement appeared in England. Simple guys-hooligans looked like students of prestigious schools. They wore draped jackets, pipe trousers and shirts with white collars. Teddy-boys were replaced in 1958 by "mods" (English modos), which somewhat resembled "tedds", but their style was more moderate and distinguished by minimalism. In the 60s, mods were replaced by new subcultures – aggressive punks and skinheads and pretentious hippies.

H&M

The H&M brand was founded by Erling Persson back in 1947 in Sweden

The first store where this women's clothing was sold was called Hennes. It cannot be said that sales were high, but demand did not fall for 20 years.

After that, the owner bought another store - Mauritz Widforss. Clothing and accessories for fishing and hunting, partly tourism, were sold here.

Bershka

Bershka was founded in 1998 as a new brand of the Spanish Inditex group.

Bershka sees itself as a beacon in the fashion world, focused on an increasingly demanding audience, and in just two years the company has united hundreds of stores under its brand.

Bershka's clients are young people who love adventures, are well versed in new trends, love music, actively use social networks and new technologies.
